Frequently Asked Quetions

Have a question? Do we have it listed below? If not please go to our contact form!

Can you use a stroller on the trails in the park?

Unfortuantly it is not recommened as most areas are quite rocky, and require stepping over large rocks.

Can you kayak to the waterfall?

Yes, it is possible.

Is it possible to drive to the waterfall?

No, although it is not an extensive hike. It is only around a 5-8 minute walk.

Are dogs allowed on the hiking trails?

Yes, although they must be on a leash and everything must be picked up.

Can you swim here?

Yes you may swim. These are designated swimming spots as well as swimming in the lake itself. Although please keep in mind to wear a life jacket or protective gear. This ensures safety on all parties parts!

Is there an entrance fee associated with attendance?

For hiking and entrace, there is not fee. Although if you plan to camp, you will have to pay a fee.

Do campsites include a grill?

Yes, there are waist high grills, and a fire ring with a grill attached!

Is fishing allowed here, and how much?

Yes you are allowed to fish, you can either making a boat rental or bring your own. Fishing lisences are required, and we have onsite bait and other necessities.
