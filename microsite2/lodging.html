Lodging Information

Here is some information on the lodging and different amenities offered at lake catherine state park.

Lake Catherine State Park

Lodging Amenities

2 Bedroom ADA — Cabin #: 14

ADA accessible, spa tub (not accessible), roll-in shower (accessible). Wheelchair accessible-large fishing dock. Wood burning fireplace. The charge for each additional person is $13 per day. Children age 12 and under stay free.

Check Availability

Duplex Cabins — Cabin #: 10, 11, 12, 13

One queen bed in the bedroom, two hide-a-bed sofas or futon in the living room. Roomy. 12 & 13 have wheelchair accessible showers but are not considered ADA cabins. Wood-burning fireplace. The charge for each additional person age 18 and up is $15 per day. Children age 17 and under stay free. (These are dog-friendly cabins and require additional fees if you are bringing your dog.)

Check Availability

Duplex Cabins (interconnected) — Cabin #: 17, 18, 19, 20

17-18 and 19-20 have an interconnecting door. 2 queen beds in BR and 1 hide a bed in LR. Wood- burning fireplace. The charge for each additional person is $15 per day for 18 and up. Children age 17 and under stay free.

Check Availability

Fourplex — Cabin #: 6, 7, 8, 9

Queen bed in the bedroom and two hide-a-bed sofas in the living room. Smaller units. NOTE: cabins 6 through 9 only have showers. The charge for each additional person is $15 per day. Children age 17 and under stay free.

Check Availability

Rustic Cabins — Cabin #: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5

Queen bed in BR and hide a bed in LR. 1,2,3,5 on nat. hist register. Wood burning fireplace. Cabin #4 is dog-friendly, please contact the park to reserve this cabin specifically and there are additional charges to bring your dog. The charge for each additional person is $15 per day. Children age 17 and under stay free.

Check Availability

Rustic Studio Duplex — Cabin #: 15, 16

Beautifully converted CCC fishing barrack overlooking a quiet cove. These studio cabins have a maximum occupancy of 2 people each.

Check Availability
